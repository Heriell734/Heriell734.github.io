About Me

You have just found my About Me page. Congratulations! I am a student
attending the University of Rhode Island. I had switched into the Computer Science B.S
major after being a Health Science major for a year. I grew up in Cranston, RI and attended
Cranston East High School. When I was a kid, I played baseball and that was up until
middle school where I then joined a dance academy. We went on to win some competitions
and I found it really enjoyable! I even joined two of the dance teams on campus my
freshman year.

Life On Campus!

Like most students, I started out my college life by living on campus. I lived in a dorm,
shared trials and tribulations with my roommate, had a part-time job on campus, and joined
a few of the clubs here. Both of the clubs that I joined were dancing clubs where I could
continue doing Hip-Hop. The first one that I had joined was URI Dance Company. I am no longer
a part of that club, but I did have a good time there as everyone there is so nice. The second
club that I joined was the eXposure club. We did more that just Hip-Hop as it was a way to show
different styles of dancing throughout the world with a heavy focus on caribbean and afro dance
styles. I am currently the assistant choreographer where I help any person struggling to learn the
or I help the choreographers when they are stuck on creating the dance as well. The picture to the
right is the eXposure logo!
